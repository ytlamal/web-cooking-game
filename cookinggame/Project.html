 1. Once the game start, player need to prepare corresponding food asked by customers.

 2. On the right-hand side, player can check the food needed and the time left for each of the customers.

 3. Player has three HP. If player didn't finish a dish during the left time of a customer, player will lose 1 HP.

 4. To check what kind of raw material need to be ready in order to get the finished product, player can look at the "Recipe" on the left-top.

 5. To cook raw material, click materials on the left-bottom and then click stoves at the central, different material need different time to be ready.

 6. After raw material cooked, click the cooked material on the stove and it will automatically be placed on the right-buttom.

 7. For some dishes, player have to click different materials required by that dish and click the "Combine" button to get the finished product.

 8. Player can also click the leaderboard to check the top-10 players in the server.
